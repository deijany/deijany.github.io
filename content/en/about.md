passion_title: "Research Interests"

passion_text: >-
  My main research interests include estimation and synchronization, hardware-constrained signal processing, linearization of non-ideal ADC/DAC response, and equalization of frequency-response distortions. I am particularly interested in methods that balance theoretical rigor, robustness, and computational efficiency for deployment on real hardware and at scale. A recurring theme in my work is the use of learning-inspired and unsupervised methods to discover structure, tune models, and improve performance when labeled data is limited.
